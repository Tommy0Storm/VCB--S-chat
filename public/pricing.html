Frequently asked questions

How do you define a conversation?

A conversation includes all exchanges within a 30-minute window of activity. After 30 minutes of inactivity the session resets and a new conversation begins.

Is my data kept within South Africa?

Yes. All data is processed and stored locally to help you comply with POPIA and other regional privacy regulations.

Can I switch tiers later?

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle and unused pay-as-you-go credits stay in your account.
